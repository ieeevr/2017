Registration

 

Registration for IEEE VR 2017 is now open!

Click here to register!

Registration Types

Full Week: USB Proceedings, all 3DUI and VR sessions (Sat-Wed), Tutorials & Workshops (Sat-Sun), 3DUI Social Event and 3DUI Contest (Sat Night), Exhibitors Reception (Sun Night), University Reception (Mon night), VR Banquet (Tue night)

VR Only: USB Proceedings, VR sessions (Mon-Wed), Exhibitors Reception (Sun night), University Reception (Mon night), VR Banquet (Tue night)

3DUI, Tutorials, & Workshops Only: USB Proceedings, All 3DUI sessions (Sat-Sun), Tutorials & Workshops (Sat-Sun), 3DUI Social Event and 3DUI Contest (Sat night)

One Day Conference Only: USB Proceedings, Access to Technical Sessions, Exhibits, and Breaks for One Day Only. If applicable, a ticket to the day’s social event must be purchased separately.

One Day Exhibits Only: Access to the Exhibits Venue, Research Demos, and Breaks for One Day Only, Does not include assess to technical sessions. If applicable, a ticket to the day’s social event must be purchased separately. If multi-day exhibits access is desired, additional one day passes must be purchased onsite.
